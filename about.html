At DesignForward, our aim is to provide excellent design solutions, in graphic design and also web development solutions, for each of our clients so that they can build meaningful, creative and effective brands and visual solutions. We truly believe in the power of great design and we pride ourselves on our commitment to make the design process enjoyable and collaborative for each of our clients and individual projects.

Gemma Harrison

CREATIVE DIRECTOR

Gemma has over 23 years of experience building brands and started DesignForward in 2014 after her national and global success in a large graphic design company. Gemma appreciates for the niche values of each projects, large and small, and has successfully built up effective and string relationships.

Gemma lives with her partner in the outskirts of Dublin, has 2 adult children and loves jigsaw puzzles.

Adam White

GRAPHIC DESIGNER

Adam graduated from Visual Communications in NCAD in 2009 and has since worked as a Graphic Designer in Australia, New York and Dublin, Adam ensures that everything DesignForward produce with clients is visually appealing, creative and fresh.

Rebecca Dobson
